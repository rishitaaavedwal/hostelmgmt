Our Comfortable Rooms

We offer a variety of room configurations to suit your needs, all designed for comfort and productivity.

Single Occupancy

Perfect for students who prefer privacy and a quiet study environment.

  • Private Bathroom
  • Study Desk & Chair
  • Wardrobe
  • High-Speed Wi-Fi

Double Occupancy

Ideal for friends or those who enjoy shared living, offering a balanced environment.

  • Shared Bathroom
  • Two Study Desks & Chairs
  • Individual Wardrobes
  • High-Speed Wi-Fi

Triple Occupancy

A cost-effective option for students looking for a communal living experience.

  • Shared Bathroom
  • Three Study Desks & Chairs
  • Individual Lockers
  • High-Speed Wi-Fi
