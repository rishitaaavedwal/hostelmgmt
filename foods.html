Hostel Fee Structure

Below you will find the detailed breakdown of our hostel fees for various room types and facilities. Please note the payment deadlines and policies.

Annual Fee Details

Category Amount (INR) Description
Admission Fee ₹5,000 One-time fee for new admissions.
Security Deposit ₹10,000 Refundable deposit, subject to deductions for damages.
Single Occupancy Room ₹80,000 / year Per academic year, includes basic amenities.
Double Occupancy Room ₹65,000 / year Per academic year, per student.
Triple Occupancy Room ₹50,000 / year Per academic year, per student.
Mess Charges (Optional) ₹40,000 / year For all meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner).
Laundry Service (Optional) ₹5,000 / year Unlimited washes, subject to fair usage policy.
Gym Access (Optional) ₹3,000 / year Access to fully equipped gymnasium.

Important Notes:

  • All fees are subject to revision annually.
  • Payment can be made in two installments (50% at admission, 50% by October 31st).
  • Late payment fines apply as per hostel policy.
  • For any queries regarding fees, please contact the hostel administration office.
  • Refund policy for withdrawal is available upon request.

Nutritious and Delicious Meals

Our hostel mess provides a balanced and varied diet to ensure the well-being of all residents.

Our Meal Plans

We offer comprehensive meal plans covering breakfast, lunch, and dinner, prepared with fresh, local ingredients. Special dietary requirements can be accommodated upon request and prior notification.

  • Breakfast: 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Lunch: 12:30 PM - 2:00 PM
  • Dinner: 7:30 PM - 9:00 PM

  • Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian options available daily.

Our Vibrant Dance Studio

Unleash your passion for dance in our dedicated studio, equipped for various dance forms and practices.

Studio Features

Our dance studio provides an inspiring environment for all levels of dancers, from beginners to advanced.

  • Sprung Wooden Flooring
  • Full-Length Mirrors
  • High-Quality Sound System
  • Adjustable Lighting
  • Air-Conditioned Environment

Activities & Classes

The dance studio is available for individual practice, group rehearsals, and organized classes.

Regular Classes Offered:

  • Bollywood Dance
  • Hip-Hop
  • Contemporary Dance
  • Zumba & Aerobics
  • Classical Indian Dance (Optional workshops)

Studio Booking:

Students can book the studio for personal practice or group rehearsals through the hostel office. Priority is given to organized classes and hostel events.
